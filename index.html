Global Terrorism Visualization [Slides]

Introduction

Group Member: Yiran Cao, Jiaqi Zhang, Zihao Wang

Source code and data is available here

1. Move the slider to display information for a particular year. The attacks for that year are projected onto the map by latitude and longitude values.

2. View attacks from different perspectives: select a different view from the dropdown. You can view the attacks by their attack type, the kind of weapons used and the type of targets.

3. Zoom and pan the map for better viewing. Hover for tooltips.

4. Click on the play/pause button to trigger auto-play

Missing data:

Some attacks are missing their latitude and longitude values in the data from GTD. As a result it is not possible to project the location of these attacks on the map.
